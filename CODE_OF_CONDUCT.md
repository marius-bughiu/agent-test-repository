# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We — maintainers, contributors, and the coding agents that consume this repository — pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the agents that rely on this benchmark
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Submitting scorecards that have been manipulated or misrepresent an agent's actual run
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository, issue tracker, PRs, discussions, and any public communication where an individual represents the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a **confidential** issue labeled `conduct` or by contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
